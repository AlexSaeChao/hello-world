North America

We just really like food from around the world. Mac and cheese, fried chicken, and apple pies are popular North American dishes due to their comforting taste, long-standing cultural significance, and widespread availability. These dishes have become staples in American cuisine and are enjoyed at family gatherings, social events, and special occasions.
